Our Concept

Kala-Kriti is an innovative platform dedicated to connecting art enthusiasts with the vibrant world of Modern and Contemporary Art. Whether you are a seasoned collector, an aspiring artist, or someone curious about the art realm, Kala-Kriti offers an inclusive space for exploration, discovery, and transactions.

Our services are designed to provide seamless experiences in browsing, buying, and selling art pieces. We also keep you informed about the latest art news, upcoming events, and exhibitions, fostering a rich community centered around artistic passion and excellence.

Experience

Our experts have extensive experience working in the art world (galleries, auction houses, appraisal companies) and check each sales request before it is posted on our website.

Transparency

There are no intermediary agents; sellers and buyers are in direct contact. The price is displayed as well as the shipping costs. Buyers leave comments and reviews about the sellers.

Reliability

We carefully follow each step of the sales and purchasing process to provide you with the utmost assistance while offering you a secure online payment method.
