Delve into the biographies of creative minds from around the world. Each artist brings a unique style and vision, making Kala-Kriti an eclectic hub of Modern and Contemporary Art.

Featured Artists

Meet the brilliant creators behind the masterpieces.

Raja Ravi Varma

A celebrated Indian painter known for bringing mythological stories to life with his blending of European techniques and Indian sensibilities.

Country: India

Specialization: Oil Painting

M.F. Husain

One of the most iconic figures of modern Indian art, M.F. Husain's vibrant paintings capture the ethos of Indian culture.

Country: India

Specialization: Contemporary Painting

Ram Kumar

A master of abstract landscapes, Ram Kumar is known for his evocative paintings that depict human emotions through color and form.

Country: India

Specialization: Abstract Art

Tyeb Mehta

Known for his bold, expressive lines and striking use of color, Tyeb Mehta explored themes of restraint and chaos in modern society.

Country: India

Specialization: Modern Painting

Arpita Singh

A prominent female artist whose works often depict women's lives in modern India, blending bright palettes with narratives of everyday life.

Country: India

Specialization: Figurative Art

S.H. Raza

Famous for his iconic Bindus and geometric abstracts, Raza fused Indian cosmology with Western modernism to create universal visual languages.

Country: India/France

Specialization: Abstract Geometric Art

F.N. Souza

A founding member of the Progressive Artists' Group, Souza's edgy style and provocative themes redefined modern Indian art.

Country: India/UK

Specialization: Expressionist Art
